Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

Spendly is built around a simple idea: your financial data belongs to you. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect basic information such as your name and email address. When you use the service, we store the expense entries you choose to log — including the amount, category, date, and any description you provide.

We also collect limited technical data, such as your IP address and basic device information, in order to keep the service secure and to diagnose issues.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data you provide solely to operate Spendly — to display your expenses, generate summaries, and keep your account working across sessions. We do not use your expense data to build advertising profiles, and we do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

Aggregated, non-identifying statistics (for example, the number of users who added an expense in a given month) may be used to improve the service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers and transmitted using industry standard encryption. Account passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data and expense entries are removed from our active systems, except where retention is required by law.

You may also export or delete your data at any time from your account settings.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly relies on a small number of trusted third party providers to run the service, such as hosting infrastructure and email delivery. These providers receive only the information needed to perform their function and are bound by confidentiality and data protection obligations.

We do not embed third party advertising or analytics trackers that build cross-site profiles.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, want to request a copy of your data, or wish to delete your account, please contact us at support@spendly.app. We aim to respond to all requests within a reasonable timeframe.
